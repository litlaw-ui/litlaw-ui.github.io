whoami

My name is Matthew Toms-Zuberec, and I’m the author of this site. I made it to help share information I come across and ideas or thoughts I have.

I am (currently) an independent security researcher and reverse engineer, and am pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I have already held multiple entry level positions, and am working towards different certifications in the information security industry.

I have a strong background in development, both web and software, which led me to branch into security.

We all know how hard is to keep up to date in the infosec world, especially since things move fast and security and threats are ever changing. My goal is to share new information and concepts, and to bring a new perspective to managing risk and the vast world of security.

This site includes:

  • light/dark mode, depending on your preferences
  • great reading experience thanks to Inter UI font, made by Rasmus Andersson
  • nice code highlighting thanks to PrismJS

So, there you have it… enjoy!
